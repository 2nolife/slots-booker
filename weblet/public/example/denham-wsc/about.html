Denham WCS

This application demos a water ski club with one lake, different activities and pricing.

  1. You can buy, view and cancel your slots.
  2. Payment is done with user's credit and credit is updated with PayPal (sandbox) account.
  3. Some of the slots may already be booked or reserved.
  4. You can book a slot 14 days in advance from 8:00, bookings get frozen on a start date at 0:00.
  5. You can cancel a slot not later than 2 days prior to its start date.
  6. Club opening times vary on working days and weekends.
  7. Prices vary per activity, members and non-members.
  8. Activities include: Slalom, Wakeboard and Ringo for 1 or 2 people.
  9. Moderator actions can be performed through the Control Panel (for now).
  10. User can see her bookings and refund statements.
  11. More features: anonymous users can see the schedule, maximum bookings per day, booking notes (to be added), sign in with email (to be added), sign out (to be added), registration (to be added).
  12. Bug: statements should display all user payments, not just booked slots.

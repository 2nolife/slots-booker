Westfield Cinema

This application mimics a cinema with four movie halls, different seat types and pricing.

  1. You can buy, view and cancel your tickets.
  2. Tickets require payment from user's balance.
  3. Some of the tickets may already be sold.
  4. You can buy tickets 7 days ahead from today.
  5. Schedules vary on working days and weekends.
  6. Prices vary per movie, seat and screen size.
  7. Seat prices and types: regular (adult, child), VIP, double.
